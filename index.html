Who we are

Cook Collective is a 6500sq. feet shared kitchen serving your small business commissary kitchen needs.

Our goal is to provide space for a community of like minded entrepreneurs, where we can problem-solve, support and grow together.

We strive to create a global collective of diverse food businesses to supply the world one great meal at a time!

What we offer


Cook Collective has 8 customizable preparation
stations, fully licensed and equipped.


We offer Station Rentals by the Shift and monthly storage options for dry, cold and frozen items.


7.5 hour Shifts: AM Shift [8:00AM-3:30PM] PM Shift [4:00PM-11:30PM] Overnight Shift [12AM-7:30AM]


Our stations are ample, refrigerated and have plenty of storage.


Memberships start as low as 2 shifts per month.


Choose a plan from 1 to 7 shifts a week. Prices start from $195 per shift.
